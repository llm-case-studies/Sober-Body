🚀 Deployment Steps

Step 1: Open Netlify

Go to https://app.netlify.com (you should already be logged in)

Step 2: Create New Site

Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"

Step 3: Upload Files

Instead of uploading the folder, upload the contents:

  1. Navigate to: /media/alex/LargeStorage/Projects/Sober-Body/apps/pronunco/dist/
  2. Select ALL files inside the dist folder:
    • index.html
    • assets/ folder
  3. Drag them to the Netlify upload area

Step 4: Configure

After upload, Netlify will give you a random URL like:

https://amazing-name-123.netlify.app

You can rename it to something like:

https://pronunco-teacher-test.netlify.app
